Boulder Junction Events

Head to Boulder Junction in the heart of Wisconsin’s Northwoods, and you’ll find a full calendar of events all year long, from holiday celebrations to live music to the annual Musky Jamboree in August—a weekend full of fun activities focused on northern Wisconsin’s most famous fish.

Other notable annual events include the White Deer Triathlon in May, the Boulder Junction Block Party (a fun weekly event featuring Music on Main, fishing seminars, food and more, held from June to August), the Star Spangled Jubilee on the Fourth of July, the Colorama Arts & Crafts Fair in September and the town’s Christmas Walk, held the day after Thanksgiving.

Note: Events are subject to change. Please contact the event organizer for information about a specific event.
