Minocqua Events

Find events and things to do in Minocqua and beyond right now. 

Browse Minocqua events throughout the year, from unique festivals and live music to the farmer's market and craft shows.  You may find a family tradition in the making at one of the area's annual events, like the spectacular 4th of July Celebration or Beef-A-Rama, our faMOOus fall festival.

Find lodging close to the event and finish your itinerary planning off with some great suggestions for dining, shopping, and other activities.  For even more things to do in the Minocqua area, order a Minocqua Area Destination Guide.
