Festivals & Events

You’ll find a full calendar of events all year long in Oneida County’s friendly communities, from fun Northwoods food festivals and community celebrations to arts attractions and scenic rides. Whether you’re visiting northern Wisconsin in spring, summer, fall, or winter, you’re sure to find an event that appeals to you.

Learn about some of Oneida County’s top annual events below or use the listings on this page to find upcoming events.

Featured events

Art Fair on the Courthouse Lawn – Rhinelander

See works from more than 150 Midwestern artists at this annual art fair in early June on the lawn of the historic Oneida County Courthouse in downtown Rhinelander. Learn more

Snowshoe Baseball – Lake Tomahawk

For more than 50 years, local residents and visitors alike have turned out every summer to take in these modified baseball games played with a softball, no gloves, and (you guessed it) snowshoes. Lake Tomahawk hosts games every Monday night in June, July, and August, and food and drinks are available for spectators. Learn more

Hodag Country Festival – Rhinelander

If you love country music, you need to be in Rhinelander in July for the annual Hodag Country Festival. Join 30,000 country music fans for four days of live shows by some of the biggest names in country music. Learn more about the Hodag Country Festival

Oneida County Fair – Rhinelander

Since 1896, this family-friendly event has welcomed visitors from across Oneida County, the Northwoods, and the state to take in exhibitions, enjoy rides and midway games, food vendors, entertainment, and more. Learn more about the Oneida County Fair.

Project North Festival – Rhinelander

This annual event highlights live music, interactive art, and the environment to draw visitors to locations throughout Rhinelander. The two-day festival features a variety of national, state, and regional musicians at venues across the city, as well as multimedia art installations and performances and sustainable education activities. Learn more about the Project North Festival.

Beef-A-Rama – Minocqua

Beef-a-Rama™ is a celebration of everything beef. Held annually on the last weekend in September, this fun-filled festival includes the Rump Roast Run and a Parade of Beef. There’s also a special “Calf Mile” race for kids. Beef-a-Rama™ features cook-offs, games for all ages, live music and great food. Learn more about Beef-a-Rama™
