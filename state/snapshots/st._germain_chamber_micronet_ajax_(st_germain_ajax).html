Events

Around St. Germain

St. Germain, WI is home to a full calendar of events that highlight the spirit and charm of Wisconsin’s Northwoods. Whether you’re visiting for summer music festivals, fall craft fairs, winter snowmobile races, or spring fishing tournaments, there’s always something to celebrate. Our events bring the community together and offer a chance for visitors to experience the authentic Northwoods lifestyle.

No matter the season, you’ll find exciting events in St. Germain, WI for families, couples, and outdoor lovers. From the famous Monday Flea Market to holiday parades and community dinners, these gatherings are the heart of our town. Check our events page regularly and plan your trip around one of St. Germain’s signature celebrations—you won’t want to miss the fun!
